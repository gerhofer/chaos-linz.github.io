Local Chaos Communication Congress

Due to the current COVID situation we have another year without the Chaos Communication Congress. On events.ccc.de people were encouraged to have local events, so that is what we decided to do and we found a great location for it. The Grand Garage is a makerspace in Linz that will open it's doors for us between christmas and new year's eve. Austrias Chaos communities will work together to make this local CCC a welcoming, fun and safe event.

Hard Facts

  • When? 27th of December 2021 10:00 - 30th of December 2021
  • Where? Grand Garage, Peter-Behrensplatz 6, 4020 Linz

COVID-19 INFO

Due to the current situation of Corona infections and the differences in incidences between vaccinated and unvaccinated groups, only vaccinated individuals may join this event. Wearing a FFP2 mask is mandatory until you can verify your vaccination status.

To make the event as save as possible for everyone we encourage and ask you take an additional PCR test beforehand.

CALL FOR PARITCIPATION

We are looking for particpation in form of lectures, workshops and panel discussions. Our venues offers us a great stage and your lectures will be streamed to the chaos community. We would also love to create the classical ccc flair with a lot of workshops that encourage attendees to collaborate and participate.

We'll keep the CFP simple, please just drop us an E-mail to chaos@linz.coderdojo.net

GETTING THERE

By public transport

The Tabakfabrik can be reached by bus with lines 12, 25 or 27. The closest station is Lederergasse

By car

In Ludlgasse near the Tabakfabrik there is a short-term parking zone free of charge. Inside the Tabakfabrik and in surrounding parking garages there are paid parking spots. Arrival by public transport, on foot or by bicycle is recommended.
